## Post body

Most AI strategy decks I see right now are fixing the wrong problem.

They show up after the founder has decided AI is the answer. So the deck rationalizes it. Picks the tools. Maps the workflow. Estimates the savings.

Nobody asks if the team can already see the truth.

Because if they cannot, AI just speeds up a broken signal. Bad data in. Bad data out, faster. The dashboard everyone already ignores now updates every minute.

I have built AI into my own operation for two years. Inside Catalyst Works it runs my outreach, my publishing, my research, my financials. It has cut hours and made me sharper.

But none of that worked until I forced myself to look at the numbers I was avoiding. The constraint was never the tooling. It was the loop the tooling was about to amplify.

If you cannot get a straight answer from your team today, AI will not fix that.

It will just make the next miss arrive sooner.
