# PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE] ("Effective Date"), by and between [CLIENT COMPANY], a [State] [Entity Type] ("Client"), and [CLIENT NAME] ("Service Provider").

---

## 1. SERVICES

Service Provider agrees to provide professional consulting and advisory services as mutually agreed upon by the parties in writing. The scope, timeline, and deliverables shall be detailed in separate statements of work or project briefs issued by Service Provider and accepted by Client.

---

## 2. FEES AND PAYMENT

Client agrees to pay Service Provider a total engagement fee of [TOTAL FEE] for services rendered under this Agreement. Payment shall be made according to the following schedule:

- 50% upon execution of this Agreement
- 50% upon completion of services

Invoices are due within thirty (30) days of receipt. Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.

---

## 3. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until completion of services, unless earlier terminated. Either party may terminate this Agreement with [X] days' written notice to the other party. Upon termination, Client shall pay Service Provider for all services performed through the termination date on a pro-rata basis.

---

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary information, trade secrets, or sensitive business information disclosed during the engagement. This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years.

---

## 5. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

**5.1 Good-Faith Resolution.** The parties agree to attempt good-faith resolution of any dispute arising from or relating to this Agreement before pursuing legal action. The parties shall meet within fourteen (14) days of written notice of a dispute to attempt resolution.

**5.2 Binding Arbitration.** If the parties cannot resolve the dispute within thirty (30) days of the initial meeting, the dispute shall be resolved by binding arbitration administered by JAMS (Judicial Arbitration and Mediation Services) or, if JAMS is unavailable, the American Arbitration Association (AAA), in Salt Lake City, Utah. The arbitration shall be conducted in accordance with the applicable rules of the selected arbitration organization. Each party shall bear its own costs, and the parties shall split the arbitrator's fees equally.

**5.3 Attorney Fees.** Except as provided herein, each party shall bear its own attorney fees and costs. Notwithstanding the foregoing, if a party proves by clear and convincing evidence that the other party engaged in fraud in connection with this Agreement, the prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney fees and costs from the non-prevailing party.

**5.4 Equitable Relief.** Nothing in this section shall prevent either party from seeking equitable relief (including injunctive relief) in a court of competent jurisdiction to prevent irreparable harm.

---

## 6. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR

Service Provider is an independent contractor and not an employee of Client. Service Provider is responsible for all payroll taxes, insurance, and other statutory obligations.

---

## 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages arising from this Agreement, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.

**8.1 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with any statements of work, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements.

**8.2 Amendments.** This Agreement may only be amended in writing and signed by both parties.
