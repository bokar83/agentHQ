### USAGE NOTES

- **Personalize the opener** when you have context on the recipient's company, industry, or a recent trigger event (new leadership, earnings miss, acquisition, etc.). Example swap: *"Saw that [Company] just wrapped a major acquisition, that's exactly the kind of moment where margin discipline tends to slip through the cracks."*
- **Subject line:** "Quick question about your margins" performs well because it implies a specific, answerable question without revealing everything upfront.
- **The 3–7% figure** is a credibility anchor. If you have a tighter range from your own client data, use that instead, specificity wins.
- **Dual CTA (call or sit-down)** reduces friction. The prospect picks the format; you just need a yes.
- **Word count:** ~130 words. Intentionally short. Respect their time and they're more likely to read the whole thing.
