## RESEARCH ANALYSIS: PROVEN FRAMEWORKS

### Logan Gott's 8-Part LinkedIn Funnel (Zero Ad Spend)

**1. Daily Authority-Building Content**
- Post 1x daily with consistent messaging
- Mix of educational, case study, and thought leadership
- 80% value-first, 20% promotional

**2. Profile Optimization as Landing Page**
- Headline: Clear value proposition + target audience
- About section: Problem-solution framework
- Featured section: Case studies, demos, lead magnets
- Experience: Results-oriented descriptions

**3. Strategic Connection Requests**
- 20-30 personalized requests daily
- Target ICP with specific pain points
- No sales pitch in first message

**4. Value-First Messaging Approach**
- 3-touch sequence over 14 days
- Touch 1: Value/resource
- Touch 2: Case study/relevance
- Touch 3: Soft call-to-action

**5. Lead Qualification System**
- BANT framework (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
- Qualification questions in first conversation
- Disqualify early to save time

**6. Automated Follow-Up Sequences**
- 7-day automated follow-up
- Value-add at each touchpoint
- Clear next-step CTA

**7. Conversion Optimization**
- A/B test messaging
- Track what content converts
- Optimize based on data

**8. Analytics and Iteration**
- Weekly metrics review
- Track lead sources and quality
- Adjust strategy based on performance

### Industry Benchmarks
- **Connection Acceptance Rate:** 40-60% (personalized)

- **Lead-to-Meeting Rate:** 15-25%
- **Meeting-to-Close Rate:** 20-40%
- **Cost Per Lead:** $0 (organic) vs. $50-200 (paid)
