## CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW

**Objective:** Cold outreach to CRM leads across two segments, Professional Services (Legal, Accounting, Marketing Agencies) and Trades (HVAC, Plumbing, Roofing), introducing the free AI Governance Model Tool as the entry point, with a consulting upsell for companies that need implementation support.

**Sequence Logic:**
- Email 1 (Day 1): Problem-first cold open. No ask for a meeting. Just a sharp observation and a free tool.
- Email 2 (Day 4): Social proof / consequence framing. One soft CTA.
- Email 3 (Day 9): Final follow-up. Direct ask. Easy exit.

**Personalization Variables:**
- `{{first_name}}`, Contact's first name
- `{{company}}`, Company name
- `{{industry}}`, Their industry (legal, accounting, HVAC, etc.)
- `{{industry_pain}}`, Industry-specific AI risk (see segment notes below)
- `{{sender_name}}`, Your name
- `{{tool_url}}`, Link to the free governance tool

## Sending Guidelines

- Send from a real inbox, not a mass platform, for first contact
- Personalize at minimum: first name, company name, industry reference in Email 2
- Do not send all three emails in the same week, respect the spacing
- Track open rates by subject line variant; clicks on tool URL are your primary conversion metric
- A reply, even a "not interested", is a win. It means the email landed.

## The Upsell Path

**Free tool** → generates basic governance framework
**Consulting engagement** → custom framework mapped to their specific workflows, client types, regulatory environment, and team structure

The emails don't sell the consulting. They sell the free tool. The free tool sells the consulting.
