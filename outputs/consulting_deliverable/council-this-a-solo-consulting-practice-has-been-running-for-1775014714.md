The constraint is not visibility, it is a vague, underpriced offer with no sales process behind it. At $3k/month the consultant is likely doing small, one-off projects at low rates for whoever shows up, then mistaking the resulting quiet for a marketing problem. The fix is surgical: define one specific, high-value transformation for one specific buyer type, price it at $5k–$15k per engagement, build a minimal outbound motion (direct outreach + referral asks to existing happy clients), and track every lead through to close. Content creation should stop entirely until the offer converts at a higher price point through direct channels.
