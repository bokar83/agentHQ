## What This Is

Signal Works is an AI Presence Agency that makes local businesses visible when potential customers ask AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini) for recommendations in their niche and city.

This is NOT a web design agency. NOT an SEO agency. The product is AI presence infrastructure, the combination of technical setup, content, and ongoing optimization that gives a local business the best possible chance of appearing in AI-generated recommendations.

The website is the infrastructure. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the service. The monthly AI Visibility Report is the proof. The consulting bridge (Signal Session / Catalyst Works) is the upsell.

---

## Why It Works Right Now

1. **88% of AI citations come from pages NOT in Google's top 10.** Businesses with weak Google SEO can still appear in AI results if optimized correctly. This fact surprises every client.
2. **Most local businesses have GPTBot blocked in robots.txt.** This makes them automatically invisible to ChatGPT and Perplexity. Fixing it takes 10 minutes and is a fast visible win.
3. **Bing Places is unclaimed by most local businesses.** ChatGPT uses Bing to find local businesses. Unclaimed = invisible. Claiming and optimizing takes 30 minutes. Immediate impact.
4. **Nobody is selling this to local businesses yet.** The 17+ YouTube videos reviewed did not mention GEO. This is a first-mover window.
5. **Boubacar's background is unique.** GE operational DNA + AMPLIFY diagnostic framework + multilingual (English, French, Arabic, others) + Catalyst Works credibility = a combination no competitor can replicate.

## The AI Visibility Score

The entry hook. Generated before every cold email is sent.

**What it measures (0-100):**
- +25: Business mentioned in ChatGPT answer for "[niche] in [city]"
- +25: Business mentioned in Perplexity answer for "[niche] in [city]"
- +25: robots.txt allows GPTBot and PerplexityBot (no blocking)
- +25: Bing Places listing is claimed and populated

**What it does NOT claim:** It does not predict all AI behavior. It measures infrastructure readiness and known citation sources. This is defensible because it measures real, fixable things.

**Why it works as a hook:** Most businesses score 0-25. Showing them their score in the subject line of a cold email creates immediate curiosity. The score makes the problem tangible and the fix obvious.

## The Sales Call (30 Minutes)

### Before the call (5 min)
- Pull up their AI score and breakdown
- Open their website in a browser tab
- Open the demo site for their niche in another tab
- Have Stripe + HelloSign + Calendly links ready to paste

### Discovery (8 min)
- "How do new customers find you today. mostly referrals, Google, something else?"
- "Do you know if your business comes up when someone asks ChatGPT for a [niche] in [city]?"
- "Walk me through what your website does for you today."

### Demo (9 min)
- Share screen
- Show their AI score: "Here is what I found when I ran your visibility check"
- Show the specific gaps: robots.txt blocked / Bing unclaimed / not appearing in AI answers
- Show a competitor who IS appearing: "This is what being AI-visible looks like"
- Show the demo site: "Here is what I built to show what a fixed version looks like"
- Show schema markup in page source: "This is what AI tools actually read"

### Close (8 min)
- "Based on what you told me, I think the [Starter / Pro / Complete] package fits best"
- State price directly: "That is $[X] setup and $[Y] per month"
- Stop talking. Wait.
- If yes: Send contract + setup fee payment link before they hang up
- If "need to think about it": Send both links anyway. "I'll leave the demo live for 48 hours."
- If price objection: "How much is one new customer worth to you? At $[monthly], you need [X] extra customers per year to break even. After that it's margin."

### After the call (5 min)
- Update Supabase lead status
- Set 48-hour follow-up if contract not signed
