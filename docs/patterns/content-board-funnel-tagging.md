# Content Board Funnel-Stage Tagging (spec, not live)

Created 2026-05-14 from Lieben LinkedIn playbook absorb (Step 6, the genuine-gap step). See `docs/reviews/absorb-followups.md` 2026-05-13 Lieben row and `skills/ctq-social/references/linkedin-craft.md` Part 9 Step 6 for context.

This file is a **doc-spec**, not a live Notion schema change. Reason: a Notion property change is a Phase-2 wire-up that should only fire after 30+ CW or SW posts ship with this taxonomy applied in draft notes and conversion data justifies the structural change.

## Scope

- CW (Catalyst Works) and SW (Signal Works) content only.
- AIC (AI Catalyst) and other Studio channels keep their existing arc-phase / narrative-pacing tagging. Different funnel mechanics — video-content moves audiences differently than B2B-services LinkedIn copy.

## The 3 stages

### TOFU — Top of Funnel

**Goal:** awareness + pattern recognition. Reader feels seen, recognizes their own pain, screenshots or follows.

**CW examples:** "the moment you realized you were the bottleneck"; "the operator-level mistake that hides as a discipline problem"; "5 patterns of services owners who scale past $5M".

**SW examples:** "why your roofing-co site keeps losing leads to the same competitor"; "the 3 trust signals every $3M-plus services site has that yours probably doesn't".

**CTA:** none, or "save this", or a no-friction follow / share. Never a Calendly link.

### MOFU — Middle of Funnel

**Goal:** trust + frame. Reader is already aware of the pain; now they evaluate whether you understand it at depth and whether you're the right shape to help.

**CW examples:** "the 4-step Catalyst Growth Engine I run for $1M-$10M services operators"; "what 90 days of fractional-CMO looks like before any retainer"; "the discovery-call question I open with that filters fit in 90 seconds".

**SW examples:** "before-and-after teardown of [niche] websites"; "what we changed about HVAC site copy that doubled form fills"; "the 3 deliverables in a Signal Session and what each one is worth alone".

**CTA:** "DM me the word audit" / "reply with your site and I'll send you the teardown" / "comment industry name and I'll tell you which template fits". Friction is low, signal is qualified.

### BOFU — Bottom of Funnel

**Goal:** booked discovery call. Reader is qualified, recognizes the offer, ready to act.

**CW examples:** Signal Session offer + price + Calendly link; case study with named-client outcome + offer to apply pattern to reader's business.

**SW examples:** rebuild offer with starting price + 2-line deliverable list + Calendly; named-client SW website + reader-CTA "want yours next".

**CTA:** explicit Calendly link, explicit price (or "from $X"), explicit deliverable.

## Tagging rule (during doc-spec phase)

Every CW + SW post drafted in Content Board includes a TOFU/MOFU/BOFU tag in the **draft notes field**, not in the post body, not in a Notion property yet.

Cadence target across a rolling 30-day window:
- TOFU: 50-70%
- MOFU: 20-30%
- BOFU: 10-20%

Mismatch in the live queue (8 TOFU drafts + 0 BOFU, OR 6 BOFU drafts back-to-back) is a signal to rebalance before pushing more drafts through.

## When this becomes a live Notion property

Trigger 1: SW publishes 30+ posts and conversion data shows a stage that overproduces relative to its conversion rate (e.g., 25 TOFU posts but TOFU-to-MOFU click-through is below 2%).

Trigger 2: Boubacar wants a Content Board view filtered by stage (queryable view requires the Notion property, not a draft-notes string).

Either trigger flips this from doc-spec to Notion schema work. The schema change ships as its own branch.

## What NOT to apply funnel tagging to

- AIC video pipeline drafts (arc-phase taxonomy already governs)
- Newsletter editorial calendar (different cadence model)
- Studio short-form video drafts (no funnel; brand-discovery moat)
- Personal posts (faith / family / off-topic): no funnel-stage, by design
