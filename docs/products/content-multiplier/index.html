Yes, if you do the voice-loading step. It takes 20 minutes once. You paste 5-10 of your past posts and a short profile. The AI reflects voice back in every output after that. Skip the voice loading and yes, it'll sound like AI.
Q2Which AI do I need?
Any modern chat AI. ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, Gemini Advanced, Grok. Paid tier, $20/month. The prompts work across all of them. We note where models differ (Claude is better for voice; ChatGPT is better for hooks; both work).
Q3Do I need to post to all 7 platforms?
No. Pick 3 to start. The kit shows you which 3 fit your business. The other 4 sit ready when you're ready to scale up. Most solo operators post to LinkedIn + X + one newsletter for the first 6 months and add platforms as they have bandwidth.

Posting consistency: week 1. Quality lift on individual posts: week 2-3. Audience growth: 3-6 months for first signal, 12 months for compounding. The kit makes the consistency possible. The platform algorithms do the rest.
