Workflow 01 · Email triage

Stop reading every email. Triage in four minutes.

1
Outcome — paste-ready inbox snapshot

Snapshot your inbox

Open your email client. Copy the subject + sender for the last 30-50 unread messages into a scratch doc. That's the input.

2
Outcome — 3 buckets + drafts

Paste this prompt into any LLM

You are my email triage assistant. Take the inbox list below and split it into 3 buckets: 1. ACTION NEEDED (under 5 min reply, do today) 2. READ LATER (informational, no action) 3. ARCHIVE / DELETE (newsletters, junk, FYIs already handled) For each ACTION NEEDED item, draft a 2-3 sentence reply I can edit and send. Inbox: [paste your 30-50 lines here]
3
Outcome — 5-12 emails closed in 30 minutes

Send the action-needed batch

Copy each draft into the reply box. Edit one line for your voice. Send. Move to the next. Don't re-read the LLM output. Trust the draft.

The LLM is not your replacement. It is your first draft. The draft is eighty percent of the work.

— Boubacar
