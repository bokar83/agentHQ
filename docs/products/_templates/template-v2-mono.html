FILE / 03 · WORKFLOW 1 / EMAIL TRIAGE

4 minutes. Not 2 hours.

Snapshot your inbox

→ paste-ready list of 30-50 unread items

Open your email client. Copy subject + sender for the last 30-50 unread messages into a scratch doc.

Paste this prompt into any LLM

→ 3 buckets + drafts for action-needed
You are my email triage assistant. Take the inbox list below and split it into 3 buckets: 1. ACTION NEEDED (under 5 min reply, do today) 2. READ LATER (informational, no action) 3. ARCHIVE / DELETE (newsletters, junk, FYIs already handled) For each ACTION NEEDED item, draft a 2-3 sentence reply I can edit and send. Inbox: [paste your 30-50 lines here]

Send the action-needed batch

→ 5-12 emails closed in 30 minutes

Copy each draft into the reply box. Edit one line for your voice. Send. Move on.

The LLM is not your replacement. It is your first draft. The draft is 80% of the work.
