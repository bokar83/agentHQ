## Color Palette

Boubacar's colors: Fulani indigo (deep blue-violet - the blue of his West African heritage, not a corporate navy), warm orange (the energy he brings into a room - people light up), green (growth, hope, the direction he's moving). Three colors that say: West African diaspora man who builds things that run while he sleeps, teaching you to do the same.

This palette should feel like NO OTHER AI channel. Every AI channel uses teal-on-dark or purple-gradient. This one wears its culture.

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|------|------|-----|-----|
| Primary | Fulani Indigo | `#2D1B8E` | Wordmark, primary CTAs, anchor color, header backgrounds |
| Secondary | Catalyst Orange | `#F26419` | Energy accent, highlight beats, subscribe button, data reveals |
| Accent 1 | Living Green | `#1DB954` | Growth indicators, positive data, "you're still standing" moments |
| Accent 2 | Pale Indigo | `#8B7FD4` | Secondary text highlights, chart fills, softer indigo moments |
| Text | Near Black | `#0A0912` | Body text - indigo-tinted black, not flat |
| Surface Dark | Deep Indigo Night | `#120B3D` | Default dark mode bg - channel lives here |
| Surface Mid | Indigo Dusk | `#1E1550` | Card backgrounds, panel fills on dark mode |
| Surface Light | Warm White | `#F8F6FF` | Light mode bg - slight indigo tint, not clinical |
| Neutral | Cool Slate | `#8A8FAA` | Captions, timestamps, secondary labels |

**Palette logic:** Fulani Indigo is Boubacar's signature color - deep, distinctive, pulls from West African textile tradition (the indigo-dyed fabric of the Fulani people). Catalyst Orange is the energy - the warmth you feel when he enters a room. Living Green is the promise of every video: *you can still thrive*. Together they're a West African flag energy without being literal about it.

**Hard rules:**
- Default mode is DARK (Deep Indigo Night bg). Light mode is secondary.
- No red anywhere - ever
- Fulani Indigo + Catalyst Orange is the power pair. Never replace either with a neutral in hero elements.
- Living Green used for POSITIVE outcomes only - never on warnings or neutral content

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| Wordmark / Channel name | Space Grotesk | ExtraBold (800) | Geometric, modern, confident - reads "built this century" |
| H1 / Episode title | Space Grotesk | Bold (700) | Same family, tighter tracking (-0.02em) |
| Body / explanation | Atkinson Hyperlegible | Regular / Medium | Dyslexia-safe. Already used in Atlas M8 dashboard - brand coherence. |
| Data / numbers / stats | JetBrains Mono | Medium (500) | Monospace for percentages, job loss figures, count-ups. Signals "this is real data." |
| Director tag line | Atkinson Hyperlegible | Italic | "Director of HR with global experience" - footer position, every long-form |

**Font pairing logic:** Space Grotesk screams "I built something." Atkinson keeps it human and accessible - this is for professionals who read fast. JetBrains Mono makes every data point feel like a terminal truth, not a talking head's claim.

## Motion Vocabulary

**Pacing principle:** Confident, direct. This is a strategist presenting findings, not a hype reel. Hard cuts are okay. Fast enough to respect the viewer's intelligence. Slow enough to let data land.

| Element | Spec |
|---------|------|
| Number count-up | 1.2s `ease-out`, JetBrains Mono, Catalyst Orange `#F26419`, lands on final value with brief hold |
| Chart build | Left-to-right bar fill, 0.8s per bar, 0.15s stagger between bars, Fulani Indigo bars, Living Green positive outlier |
| Slide transition | Hard cut left-to-right (modern, no dissolve). Occasional horizontal push on key section breaks. |
| Text reveal | Word-by-word fade, 0.06s per word stagger, entrance from bottom 8px |
| Data reveal beat | 1-frame Catalyst Orange flash before key stat lands - subconscious "attention" trigger |
| Intro sequence | "AI CATALYST" wordmark assembles letter-by-letter, 1.8s, Fulani Indigo bg, Catalyst Orange glow blooms behind final letter |
| Outro | Hard cut to end-card, no dissolve - confident close |
| Lower-third tag | "Director of HR · Global Experience" - Atkinson Italic, Pale Indigo, slides up from bottom, holds throughout long-form |

**Never use:** Lens flares, glitch effects, particle storms, robot/circuit visual clichés. This is a human strategist, not a sci-fi channel.

## Thumbnail Template Grammar

**Layout:** Dark indigo bg + bold stat or question text dominant + minimal supporting visual right side

**Color zones:**
- Dominant text: Catalyst Orange `#F26419` for shock-value stats / Warm White for question hooks
- Supporting stat: JetBrains Mono, Living Green, smaller
- Right-side visual: abstract chart, person silhouette, or icon - never stock photo faces
- Bottom accent line: Catalyst Orange, 6px, full width

**Text rules:**
- Max 6 words. Ideally 3-4.
- Always a number OR a question - never a generic topic ("AI jobs" → bad / "42% gone by 2027?" → good)
- Space Grotesk ExtraBold, min 72px
- No more than 2 text elements

**Do not:** Clickbait fear thumbnails. No screaming emoji. No red warning triangles. Calm authority, not panic.
