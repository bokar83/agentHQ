## Bottlenecks (ranked)

1. **Quality video content creation.** Producing video that actually meets the bar for YouTube monetization and viewer retention. Today's manual process is the constraint; without automation help here, scaling beyond 1-2 channels is impossible.
2. **Content topic relevance.** Knowing what to post about. Some topics come from Boubacar; some come from Studio's own research (trend scout, niche scrape, etc.). The research half is partially-built but not yet the daily fuel source it needs to be.

Cost-per-week is currently NOT a constraint (irrelevant per Boubacar's note 2026-04-25).

## Channel warm-up requirement

New channels need a warm-up period before aggressive posting / monetization. Algorithm-side: posting too much too fast on a fresh YouTube channel triggers spam suppression; X has rate limits and shadow-ban heuristics. Studio cadence MUST account for this. **Research warm-up best practices per platform before launching a new channel** (open task).
