## 4. Content Plan for Warm-Up Window

**Platforms:** IG Reels + TikTok
**Content type:** Money concepts, "what nobody told you," diaspora wealth-building moments
**Warm-up window:** 30 days from account creation

### IG Warm-Up Protocol (Days 1-30)

| Phase | Days | Actions | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Silence | 1-3 | Zero posts. Follow 5-10 first-gen / personal finance / diaspora accounts. Add bio (no link). | N/A |
| Soft open | 4-7 | 1 Reel/day, no hashtags, no link. 1 Story/day (stat or question card). | "Did you know?" money facts, calm tone, no CTA. |
| Ramp | 8-14 | 1 Reel/day + 1-2 Stories. Add YouTube link Day 7. 3-5 hashtags. | "What nobody told you about [topic]" format. |
| Steady | 15-30 | 1 Reel/day + 2-3 Stories. 5 hashtags max. Consistent time window. | Full concept clips → YouTube CTA. Disclaimer in caption. |

**Hashtag pool (5 max per post):** `#FirstGenWealth` `#ImmigrantMoney` `#PersonalFinance` `#BuildingWealth` `#MoneyEducation`

**YMYL guard:** Every financial-topic Reel caption ends with: *"Financial education only  -  not personal advice. Always consult a professional."*

**Never post:** Hype. Lamborghinis. "MAKE MONEY FAST." Specific stock/crypto picks. Lifestyle flex. This channel is the antidote to all of that.

### TikTok Warm-Up Protocol (Days 1-30)

| Phase | Days | Actions | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Observe | 1-7 | Zero posting. 20-30 min/day: watch personal finance, first-gen, money niche content. Follow 5 accounts/day max. | N/A |
| First posts | 8-14 | 1 video/day. 4+ hour spacing between uploads. Consistent device + IP. | 30-60s simple money explanations. Stat + clear takeaway. |
| Ramp | 15-21 | 1-2 videos/day. Niche keywords in captions. | "What nobody told you about [X]" format. Calm, clear. |
| Steady | 22-30 | 2 videos/day max. Consistent window (e.g., 8am + 7pm). | Repurpose top IG performers → TikTok. Always close with disclaimer. |

**Voice note:** Gender-neutral ElevenLabs voice `X4Lh5Ftnso6JSt25plzX` (Hunter). Short-form clips use same voice as YouTube for brand consistency. Do not mix male/female voice markers  -  keep it warm and neutral.

## 8. YMYL Compliance Note

First Generation Money is YMYL (Your Money Your Life) content. Both IG and TikTok flag financial content for additional review. To avoid suppression:
- No specific investment advice in any post
- Disclaimer in video voiceover AND caption for any money topic
- "Financial education, not personal advice"  -  spoken clearly, not rushed (per voice spec in brand bible)
- No mention of specific stocks, crypto tickers, or projected returns in short-form clips
