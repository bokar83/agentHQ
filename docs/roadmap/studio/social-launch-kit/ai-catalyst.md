## 4. Content Plan for Warm-Up Window

**Platforms:** IG Reels + TikTok
**Content type:** Short AI/career takes, "Did you know?" stats, 3-step frameworks
**Warm-up window:** 30 days from account creation

### IG Warm-Up Protocol (Days 1-30)

| Phase | Days | Actions | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Silence | 1-3 | Zero posts. Follow 5-10 AI/HR/career accounts. Add bio (no link). | N/A |
| Soft open | 4-7 | 1 Reel/day, no hashtags, no link. 1 Story/day (quote card or stat). | AI fact of the week, calm tone, no CTA. |
| Ramp | 8-14 | 1 Reel/day + 1-2 Stories. Add YouTube link Day 7. 3-5 hashtags. | Short takes, "What this means for your job" framing. |
| Steady | 15-30 | 1 Reel/day + 2-3 Stories. 5 hashtags max. Consistent time window. | Full framework clips → YouTube CTA in caption. |

**Hashtag pool (5 max per post):** `#AIandWork` `#FutureOfWork` `#CareerStrategy` `#HRLeadership` `#AIStrategy`

**Never post:** Alarmist "AI will take your job" framing (off-brand). Lamborghinis or hype. Investment claims.

| Phase | Days | Actions | Content |
