## 3. Link in Bio Strategy

**Primary destination:** YouTube channel (Under the Baobab)
**Secondary destination:** Not yet (no Linktree / landing page in M4)

**IG:** Use the single link field → YouTube channel URL
**TikTok:** Use website field → YouTube channel URL

## 4. Content Plan for Warm-Up Window

**Platforms:** IG Reels + TikTok
**Warm-up window:** 30 days from account creation

### IG Warm-Up Protocol (Days 1-30)

| Phase | Days | Actions | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Silence | 1-3 | Zero posts. Follow 5-10 accounts in African storytelling / children's content niche. Add bio (no link yet). | N/A |
| Soft open | 4-7 | 1 post/day, no hashtags, no link in bio. | Teaser clips ≤30s, no CTA. Post 1 Story/day. |
| Ramp | 8-14 | 1 Reel/day + 1-2 Stories. Add link to bio on Day 7. Add 3-5 niche hashtags max. | Short story excerpts, "hook" moments. |
| Steady | 15-30 | 1 Reel/day + 2-3 Stories. 5 hashtags max. | Story trailers → YouTube CTA in caption. |

**Hashtag cap:** 5 per post. Suggested pool: `#AfricanStorytelling` `#KidsStories` `#AfricanFolktales` `#KidsReels` `#ParentingTips`

**Never post:** Promo-first content, giveaways, or external links in the caption (IG penalizes outbound links in post body).

### TikTok Warm-Up Protocol (Days 1-30)

| Phase | Days | Actions | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Observe | 1-7 | Zero posting. Spend 20-30 min/day watching African stories, kids' content, parenting niche. Follow 5 accounts/day max. | N/A |
| First posts | 8-14 | 1 video/day. Space uploads 4+ hours apart. Same IP/device each session. | 30-60s story excerpts. No hard CTA. |
| Ramp | 15-21 | 1-2 videos/day. Add niche keywords to captions. | Story hooks, "Did you know?" parenting moments. |
| Steady | 22-30 | 2 videos/day max. Consistent daily window (e.g., 8am + 6pm). | Repurpose top IG Reel performance → TikTok. |

**Stability rules:** Same device, same IP, same approximate time of day. No bursts (3+ videos in <2 hours = bot signal). If account goes quiet, resume with 1 video/day for 3 days before increasing.
