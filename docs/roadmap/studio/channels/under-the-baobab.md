## What this channel is

A faceless animated storytelling channel with two content pillars:

**Pillar 1  -  African Folktales:** Akan, Wolof, Mande, Yoruba, Bamileke, Berber, Fulani, and more. Original African children's tales we write ourselves. Mix of cultures permitted. The brand is "the griot under the baobab" telling stories. NOT academic ethnography. Entertainment for African children, diaspora children, parents who want their kids hearing African voices.

**Pillar 2  -  Parenting Psychology & Childhood Development:** Science-backed parenting insights framed through warmth and connection, not fear. Early brain development, attachment styles, the first 3 years, African parenting approaches. Speaks to the same parent audience as Pillar 1  -  gatekeepers who want the best for their children. Tone: calm, grounded, "safety and connection over complexity."

Both pillars serve the same core audience and live under the same brand aesthetic. The griot voice works for both: a wise elder telling you something you need to know for your child.

## Audience

- African children (continent + diaspora)
- Parents looking for bedtime stories that reflect African heritage
- Black History Month / cultural curriculum buyers

## Production stack

- Script: Sonnet drafts from source material; original tales hand-prompted by Boubacar or trend-scout brief
- Voice: faceless TTS (ElevenLabs character voice OR Kai TTS via kie_media). One recurring narrator persona.
- Visuals: kie_media (seedream/4.5) for hand-styled stills; hyperframes for parallax + Ken Burns animation; light kie_media kling morphs for hero moments
- Composition: hyperframes assembles voice + visuals + captions + branded intro/outro
- Render: 16:9 long, 9:16 shorts, 1:1 IG square
- Asset storage: Drive `05_Asset_Library/under-the-baobab/<YYYY-MM-DD>/`

## Voice + visual brand (M2 will lock fully)

- Earthy palette: ochre, terracotta, deep indigo, baobab brown
- Textile motif library by region tag (kente for Akan, mudcloth for Mande, Adinkra for Akan, etc.)
- Single recurring TTS narrator with consistent vocal personality
- End-card pattern: animated baobab silhouette + brand name + subtle drum sting

## Cultural authenticity floor

- Region/tribe tag MANDATORY on every record
- Source citation field MANDATORY when retelling a published tale
- Original tales (no source) are permitted; flag them explicitly so they're not represented as historical
- Visual region-motif match enforced by image-generator prompt template (M3 deliverable)
