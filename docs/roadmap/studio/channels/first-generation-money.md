## What this channel is

Faceless personal finance education for first-generation US wealth builders. Immigrant kids, diaspora professionals, mid-career Americans who never got the financial literacy other people's families taught at home.

The framing: you grew up not learning what other kids learned at the kitchen table. The "money talk" never happened. You're not stupid; you just inherited zero financial literacy. Now you're 28, 35, 42, and figuring it out alone. We help.

**Zero connection to anything else Boubacar does.** Pure agentic test channel. No Boubacar name on it. No links to Catalyst Works, agentsHQ, boubacarbarry.com. Stands alone.

## Compliance / YMYL guard

Personal finance is YMYL ("Your Money Your Life"); YouTube algorithmically suppresses unverified claims. Mitigation:

- Educational framing only, never specific investment recommendations
- Clear "this is education not advice" disclosure on every long-form
- Numbers and rules cited (IRS publication numbers, contribution limits with year, etc.)
- No specific stock picks (Studio QA crew check 8 enforces this)
