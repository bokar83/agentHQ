### T2 — Start AI + Copy-Paste Pack ($79)

**T1 + scripts/ folder:**

**Safety/virus-scan protocol (mandatory on product page + readme):**
- All .md / .txt only. No executables. No .sh / .bat / .ps1. No npm/pip install commands.
- Files scanned with Microsoft Defender + VirusTotal pre-bundle. Scan report screenshot included as `safety/scan-report.png`.
- Statement: "These are text files only. Paste-only. Nothing runs on your machine. If a file refuses to open, your AV is over-blocking text; whitelist it."

| Platform | Strength | Weakness | Boubacar fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Gumroad | Cheapest discovery, license features | Public price-cuts feel low | T1 single PDFs only |
| Lemon Squeezy | Acts as MoR, handles VAT/GST/US sales tax 100+ countries | 5% + 50c | T1 + T2 + T3 (international) |
| Whop | Strong creator community, 2.7% + 30c | Newer brand | Studio creator products |
| Stripe + Hostinger landing | Lowest fee, full brand control, owns customer | You handle tax (Stripe Tax) | T3 + T4 only |

**Tax exposure:**
- US sales tax: nexus triggers (mostly $100K/yr or 200 txns). Below = no obligation. Stripe Tax automates once crossed.
- EU VAT: triggers from $1. LS handles; Stripe-direct does NOT.
- UK VAT: same. LS handles.
- Recommendation: T1/T2 on LS, T3/T4 on Stripe + Hostinger.

**Refund / chargeback:**
- T1: 14-day refund (LS default). Keep it.
- T2: 7-day refund OR "if you can't run them, free 15min call" guarantee.
- T3: no post-call refund. Pre-call yes.
- T4: 50/50 deposit non-refundable on first half.

**Embedded CTA pattern (mandatory in every SKU):**
- T1 page-1: Calendly-15min + intake-form + 1stGen Money link
- T1 last-page: Boubacar bio (1stGen, ex-HR, builder), photo, 3 CTAs
- T2 scripts/README-cta.md with same 3 CTAs
