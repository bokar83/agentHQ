01

Half your site is "Coming soon"

About, Gallery, and every individual service page show a placeholder. A homeowner who clicks "About Rod" or "See past projects" hits a dead end.

Why it costs leads: 80% of contractor buyers check About + portfolio before calling. Empty pages signal "not real" and they leave for JAM or Pressure Point.

Lead lift +30-50%
Fix: 2 weeks
02

Google can't see your page titles

Your homepage `<title>` tag is empty. Meta description is missing. No Open Graph tags. No schema markup. Google has no idea you're a roofer in Medford.

Why it matters for SEO/GEO: Title + meta + LocalBusiness schema are the three things Google reads first. Without them you're invisible in Maps, in local 3-pack, and in "roofer near me" searches.

Lead lift +20-40%
Fix: 1 day
03

No city pages, no local SEO

Every winning Southern Oregon roofer has a dedicated page per city: /service-areas/medford/, /ashland/, /grants-pass/, etc. You have one Service Area list buried in the footer.

Why it matters for GEO: Google ranks city + service queries (e.g. "roofer Ashland OR") by city-page relevance. No page = no rank = no call. Klamath Falls is wide open nobody owns it.

Lead lift +25-35%
Fix: 1 week
04

You're invisible in the hero

Your drone video is great the only one in the market. But your face isn't above the fold. Every top-5 competitor is faceless too. Putting Rod's portrait + signed pledge in the hero would make you the only owner-led face in Southern Oregon roofing.

Why it converts: Owner-operator trust beats brand-style trust 2:1 in residential trades. A homeowner choosing between "Elevate (Rod, with face)" and "Pressure Point (logo)" picks Rod every time.

Lead lift +15-25%
Fix: 1 photo
05

One CTA, no friction-reducer

Your only conversion path is a contact form. No published cost ranges. No calendar booking. No callback request. No "what to ask any roofer" lead magnet.

Why it converts: Sunday-night homeowners aren't ready to call yet but they'll book a Tuesday slot or grab a cost guide. Every micro-conversion is a future booked job.

Lead lift +10-20%
Fix: 3 days
02
The SEO & GEO scorecard.
What Google sees right now
Page title (`<title>`) Empty

Google shows this as the blue link in search results. An empty title = nobody clicks.

Meta description Missing

The grey text under the blue link. Without it, Google guesses often badly.

LocalBusiness schema (JSON-LD) Missing

The structured data Google reads to build your Maps card and Knowledge Panel. Without it, you don't get rich results.

Service-area pages 1 (should be 6)

JAM has 8. Pressure Point has 18. Each city page is a separate ranking opportunity.

Open Graph tags (link previews) Missing

When someone shares your site on Facebook, LinkedIn, or text, no preview image. Looks broken.

Sitemap.xml & robots.txt Unverified

How Google finds and indexes your pages. Should be auto-generated and submitted to Search Console.

Mobile page speed Unmeasured

Google ranks mobile-first. Slow load = lower rank + lower booking rate.

Google Business Profile (GBP) Linked? Verify

The single biggest local-SEO lever more important than the website itself for "near me" searches. We'd verify and optimize.
