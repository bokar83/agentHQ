## Theory of Constraints (TOC) — key concepts
- Every system has exactly one binding constraint limiting throughput
- Improving non-constraints does not improve system output
- Five focusing steps: Identify → Exploit → Subordinate → Elevate → Repeat
- The goal of a business is to make money now and in the future (Goldratt)
