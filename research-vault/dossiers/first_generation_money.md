## What Is Working Right Now

- **Identity hook dominant:** Titles that name the audience ("If your parents never talked about money, this is for you") pull strong first-10-second retention — audience self-selects hard
- **Shame-to-strategy arc:** Opening with the shame/silence around money in immigrant families, then pivoting to actionable steps — highest comment volume format in this niche
- **Index funds over stock picks:** Educational content on index investing and compound interest dramatically outperforms stock-tip content for this audience (less churn, more trust)
- **Tax angle evergreen:** "Tax strategies your parents didn't know" + "Roth IRA explained for first-gen families" consistently high search volume year-round
- **Generational wealth framing:** "Break the cycle" + "be the first" messaging outperforms individual wealth framing — community identity is the moat
- **YMYL compliance:** Every financial claim needs "this is not financial advice" + "consult a professional" — non-negotiable for monetization safety
