## What Is Working Right Now

- **Origin story format:** "The [animal/ancestor] who [unexpected action]" titles outperform "Learn about [topic]" titles 3:1 on YouTube in African diaspora niche
- **Diaspora nostalgia hook:** Videos that open with "Your grandmother knew this" or "Before GPS, before Google" pull strong watch time from 25-45 African diaspora audience
- **Moral paradox structure:** Folktales framed as "everyone was right, and that was the problem" drive comment engagement — audiences argue the moral
- **Short-form strong:** Channels like "African Folklore" and "Continent of Stories" see 3-5x higher TikTok completion rates on 60-90s myths vs. 8-10min YouTube long-form
- **Visual style signal:** Hand-drawn / watercolor aesthetic (simulated via AI) outperforms photorealistic renders for mythological content — feels authentic, not AI-generated
- **Voice tone:** Warm, unhurried narrator with slight formality ("In the time before the baobab grew roots...") outperforms fast-paced explainer tone
