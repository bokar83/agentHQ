01 -- Logo Usage
Primary: ink background (preferred)
Approved: forest green background
Approved: cream/parchment background
Hard Rules
  • Never recolor the logo. Gold + dark ink only.
  • Minimum clear space: logo height / 2 on all sides
  • Minimum display size: 80px height (web), 25mm (print)
  • Never stretch, rotate, or add drop shadows to the logo
  • Never place on a busy photographic background without a solid backing panel

Type Rules
  • All headlines: Cormorant Garamond. Never Poppins, Inter, or any sans-serif for display.
  • Body and UI copy: Lato only. Weight 300 (light) for eyebrows, 400 for body, 700 for labels/buttons.
  • French is primary. All copy written in French first, English toggle second.
  • Currency: GNF primary (en), FG secondary (fr). Format: 450 000 GNF / 450 000 FG.
  • No em dashes anywhere. Use colon or comma instead.
  • No uppercase body copy. Uppercase reserved for eyebrows, labels, nav only.
