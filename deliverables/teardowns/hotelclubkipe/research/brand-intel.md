# Brand Intel: Hotel Club de Kipe

## Brand Snapshot
- **Name:** Hotel Club de Kipe (also spelled Hotel Club de Kipé)
- **Tagline:** "Your tranquil retreat in Conakry, Guinea" / "Votre oasis à Conakry, Guinée"
- **Offer:** Boutique hotel, 13 rooms across 3 floors, amenities include pool, restaurant, bar, Wi-Fi, parking
- **CTA:** WhatsApp booking (broken -- number empty)
- **Voice:** Generic placeholder English. No authentic Guinean brand voice. Copy borrowed from a template.
- **Domain:** hotelclubkipe.com (offline at audit date 2026-05-05)

## Palette (Current)
- Orange: #fa9441
- Dark brown: #2d2212
- Cream: #f9f6f1

## Top Competitors
1. **Booking.com/Hotels.com aggregator pages** for Conakry -- dominate SERP for "hotel conakry guinea"
2. **Novotel Conakry** -- business hotel, OTA-listed, corporate positioning
3. **Hotel Camayenne** -- mid-range, Conakry, on TripAdvisor

## Craft Gap
HCK vs competitors: zero room details, no pricing, no real photos in hero (uses stock food photo), no booking engine, broken WhatsApp link, no reviews/social proof.

## Market Gap (opportunity)
- No boutique hotel brand owns "authentic Guinean hospitality" positioning online
- No hotel in Conakry has proper schema + AI-visible content
- French-speaking SEO for Guinea/Francophone West Africa is uncontested
- WhatsApp-primary booking is a strength in this market if actually wired correctly
