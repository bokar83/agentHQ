Design Direction (from audit findings)

Current orange-cream palette reads food delivery, not luxury hotel. Two credible directions:


Option A: Dark Elegant - Navy/dark teal + gold + white. Comparable to European boutique hotel standard. Serif display font (Playfair Display or Cormorant). High contrast, premium.


Option B: West African Boutique - Warm terracotta + deep green + cream + gold accents. Positions as authentic Guinean luxury. Distinctive vs. any European template look.


Recommendation: Option B. Differentiation is the market gap. No competitor owns "authentic West African boutique hotel" visually. Terracotta/green/gold with Cormorant Display for headlines is distinctive and premium without being a European clone.
