  • Budget proxy: the consultancy itself charges $497/session and has a clear ladder up to Diagnostic Sprint. Site investment to date: clean static HTML, brand-coded design tokens, GA4, Spectral + Public Sans paid Google Fonts, structured data on homepage.
  • Pain depth (craft + market gap from Phases 2-4): 7 / 10. Two structural fixes (orphan pages, local SEO), one brand-rule fix (em-dash + logo), several polish-pass items.

Medium nationally for "diagnostic AI consulting" (smbstrategyconsultants.com is direct overlap; differentiator is the productized first-touch).

  • Scope: in-place patch on the existing static site, not a rebuild.
  • Estimated dev hours: ~3 hours focused. Breakdown:
    • 30 min - Phase 2 high-severity items (real logo in header, broken nav, em-dash scrub, color-scheme dark, contrast bump, form wrap)
    • 20 min - hero photograph asset (use the kie_media generated image OR a founder portrait shot)
    • 30 min - Phase 4 high-priority SEO fixes (link to orphan pages, add meta descriptions + canonicals + JSON-LD to governance.html and ai-data-audit.html)
    • 30 min - local-SEO upgrade (areaServed cities in Organization schema, Service Areas section, Google Business Profile creation)
    • 30 min - copy polish (curly quotes, theme-color meta, apple-touch-icon variants, font preload check)
    • 30 min - QA pass (mobile sticky-nav scroll-margin fix, modal form behavior, em-dash sweep grep)
  • Lift required: low. No new infrastructure, no new build pipeline, no framework migration. Hostinger static hosting stays.
  • Asset cost: negligible. Hero image already generated (mockups/hero-proposed.png, 1.4MB). Logo already exists. Founder photo already exists.

4. Risks / red flags / what's leaking deals

  • Form submit button is outside its <form>, Enter-to-submit broken, autofill broken, screen reader announcements broken. This is conversion-critical and probably losing at least some bookings silently.
  • Two broken nav links (#offers), high-intent visitors clicking "How It Works" get nothing. Pure leak.
  • Em-dashes in JSON-LD FAQ answers, leak into Google rich snippets, breaking brand rule on the highest-visibility surface (SERP).
  • governance.html and ai-data-audit.html are orphaned, these are 2,100-word lead pages, completely invisible to navigating visitors. They're the natural destination for visitors who came in via the AI risk angle.
  • Logo absent from header, text-logotype reads as placeholder. The actual CW mark is premium and distinctive; using only the favicon underuses the brand asset.
  • No local-SEO signal, 3 of 5 scraped competitors rank for Utah queries. Catalyst Works ranks for none.
  • Closest competitor (smbstrategyconsultants.com) has cleaner visual craft, same frame, same vocabulary, light theme, premium photography, single H1, strong section hierarchy. A side-by-side compare today favors them on first impression.
