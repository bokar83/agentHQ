Verdict

Overall SEO score: 72 / 100

The homepage is exceptional on technical SEO and structured data. Organization, Person, Service, and FAQPage schemas are all present, well-formed, and aligned with the offer. Title and meta description are within ideal length, OG and Twitter cards are complete. The site is held back by: two interior pages (governance.html, ai-data-audit.html) that exist in the sitemap but are not linked from the homepage and lack basic meta tags, structured data, and H1 hygiene. There is also zero local-SEO signal, no Google Business Profile reference, no NAP markup, no city-specific landing page, despite the footer naming five cities served.

Closing the 28-point gap requires roughly 2-3 hours of focused work, almost all of which is on the two orphan pages and a local-SEO addition.
