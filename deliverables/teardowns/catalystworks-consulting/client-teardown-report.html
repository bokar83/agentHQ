The homepage is excellent. Title tag and meta description are within ideal length. Open Graph, Twitter Card, and four well-formed JSON-LD schemas (Organization, Person, Service with $497 offer, FAQPage with 7 Q&A) are present. The site is already eligible for FAQ rich results in search.

The two interior pages (governance.html and ai-data-audit.html) tell a different story. Both are 2,100 words of strong content but lack meta descriptions, canonical tags, and structured data. Worse, neither is linked from the homepage. Search engines find them via sitemap; humans cannot.

Zero local-SEO signal exists. The footer mentions Atlanta, Miami, Dubai, Conakry, Port Louis, but the Organization schema's areaServed lists only countries, not cities. Three of the five competitors rank for "AI consulting Utah" because they use explicit geographic markup; Catalyst Works has no Utah signal at all despite being Utah-based.

Top 5 SEO fixes (in priority order)

  1. Link from homepage to governance.html and ai-data-audit.html. One edit fixes the orphan-page problem.
  2. Add meta description, self-canonical, and Service or Article JSON-LD to both interior pages. ~30 min total.
  3. Upgrade Organization schema areaServed from country names to City entities. Add a Service Areas section to the homepage listing the five cities.
  4. Em-dash scrub on the FAQPage JSON-LD answers (they leak into Google rich snippets).
  5. Create a Google Business Profile (Utah service-area business) and submit catalystworks.consulting as the website.
