Architecture: where the data actually goes

Today (live on geolisted.co right now)

Form posts to https://formspree.io/f/boubacar. Lead lives on Formspree's servers until you fetch it. No score is computed. No Supabase write. No Notion mirror.

This is inconsistent with how your other two sites handle forms. boubacarbarry.com and catalystworks.consulting both post to n8n webhooks → Supabase.

Proposed (revised after pre-stage 2026-04-30)

▶
Visitor submits form on geolisted.co
▼
POST to n8n /webhook/geolisted-score (sync mode, holds connection)
▼
n8n shapes payload, POSTs to agentsHQ /score-request (X-API-Key auth)
▼
VPS route runs signal_works.ai_scorer.score_business()
◀
Returns {score, breakdown, quick_wins} in 30-60s
▼
VPS writes lead to Supabase leads (source: 'geolisted.co - Score Request')
▼
VPS calls email_builder.render_inbound_report_html() + sends via gmail_draft.send_message() (env-gated)
▼
n8n returns score JSON back to browser through the open connection
▼
Browser renders score on screen. Full report lands in inbox in parallel.

Note on architecture: NEW agentsHQ route /score-request (NOT a reuse of /inbound-lead; that route's contract is for booked-call leads with a booking_id, different shape). Email send is gated behind SCORE_AUTO_SEND env var; defaults to drafts mode for first 5 inbound scores, flip to send after manual verification.

Verdict

Move geolisted.co to n8n /webhook/geolisted-score like the other two domains. This is the same change for the same reason as the boubacarbarry.com migration on 2026-04-06. The Formspree URL on geolisted today was a marathon-day shortcut, not a strategic choice.

Will it stay consistent this time?

Yes. Three lock-ins:

  1. The webhook URL gets added to reference_website_registry.md so the next session inherits it.
  2. The endpoint name does not get renamed. v2 of the scoring adds a new endpoint and migrates; never rewrites in place.
  3. The CW Gmail sender (boubacar@catalystworks.consulting) is locked in signal_works/email_builder.py and stays.

What I need from you to start the build

  1. Confirm: move geolisted.co form from Formspree → n8n webhook (same as your other 2 sites).
  2. Confirm: scorer runs on the agentsHQ VPS as a new /api/score endpoint.
  3. Confirm: CW Gmail sender for the full-report email.
