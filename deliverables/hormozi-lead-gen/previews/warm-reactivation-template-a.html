What this is

Purpose

A warm reach-out to people you already know. Past colleagues, alumni, mutuals, ex-vendors, prior clients. Anyone with a real prior relationship, even if dormant for years.

You are not selling. You are surfacing a soft referral ask wrapped in a real catch-up. The Hormozi rule: warm activation comes before cold. You have 15 years of contacts unmined.

Send it the way you would normally talk to them

"By hand" just means: you are the one typing each message, one at a time. No automation, no merge tags, no bulk send. The channel itself is whatever fits the relationship:

  • Gmail if you used to email them.
  • LinkedIn DM if that was the medium.
  • iMessage / WhatsApp if you used to text them.

Match the prior channel. Do not switch unprovoked. The point of "by hand" is the personalization, not the delivery method.

Why each block is shaped this way

  1. Personalization hook. Must be REAL. "Hope you're well" gets deleted. A specific shared memory gets a reply.
  2. The reason line. Direct, no preamble. Names what Catalyst Works does in concrete terms (one approval loop / one handoff / 6-8% margin).
  3. "People I trust" framing. Flips the script. They're not a target; they're chosen. Lowers defenses.
  4. The ask. Referral or catch-up. Either is a yes. Hormozi: low-friction, low-pressure, two-path close.

Hormozi rules embedded

  • Big Fast Value first (the catch-up energy + concrete pattern naming)
  • ACA when they reply (Acknowledge / Compliment / Ask)
  • One ask only (intro OR catch-up, never both as separate asks)
  • No "circle back" / "leverage" / sales sludge
  • Activation order: warm before cold, every time
Why this matters

Why you specifically should send this template

  • You have 15 years of contacts (GE, Apex, three continents of services-firm work). That list is worth more than any cold email pipeline.
  • Catalyst Works has 0 paying clients. Hormozi's first activation channel is warm. You're skipping it.
  • The audit (Phase 2) flagged "no warm outreach system" as Severity 5: load-bearing failure.
  • Sending 3 messages by hand in the next 24 hours is the falsifier the Sankofa Council locked in. It tells us whether the lead-gen problem is system design (the skill helps) or volume of execution (the skill does not).

What you do with it (24-hour test)

Pick 3 names from your 1st-degree LinkedIn network. People you have an actual prior relationship with, even if dormant. Personalize each opener for real. Send via Gmail or LinkedIn DM (match whichever channel you used last with them).

  1. Open the file: lead-gen-system/templates/warm-reactivation-v1.md
  2. Pick 3 names. (Not 100. Don't list-build. Just 3.)
  3. For each: rewrite the personalization hook with a real shared memory.
  4. Send. By hand. No automation.
  5. Wait 24 hours. Report what happened.
