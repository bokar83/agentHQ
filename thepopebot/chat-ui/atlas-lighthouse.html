Hey Brandon. Quick ask. Starting outbound for the consulting practice this week. Need an accountability partner for the next 4 weeks. Asking you because you've been generous with me already and you ship work for a living, which is the muscle I'm trying to build on my consulting side. The arrangement is small. Every weekday morning at 10:05 MDT I text you a 3-4 line end-of-morning report: what message I sent, who to, what reply (if any) came in from yesterday, what I'm delivering today. You reply within an hour with anything. "Got it" works, "good push" works, a question works, silence does not. I get pinged by the absence; you keep the streak by acknowledging it. Four weeks, then we evaluate. Optional: if something landed that day (a yes, a no, a first reply, a delivered audit), I'll send a 2-line end-of-day note at 17:30 MDT so you see the actual outcome, not just the morning intent. No call needed unless you want one. If you have questions reply here, happy to talk through them by phone whenever. Otherwise reply yes and I'll send the first ping Wednesday morning. Boubacar
