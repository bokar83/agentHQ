Got asked yesterday why we still answer the phone on Sundays.

Because the people who need a roofer on Sunday are not picking up a phone for fun. They are picking it up because something is wrong. Their kid's bedroom ceiling is bowing. There is water hitting the kitchen floor. They are scared.

You answer that phone or you are not a roofer. You are a guy with a truck.

We answer.

Twenty-three years and counting.

— Bill at Acme
