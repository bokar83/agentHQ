# Cold Call Scripts — SW Prospect Calls

Source: m0h/@exploraX_ GMaps playbook absorb 2026-05-09.
Use: pull up before calling. Not agent-executed — Boubacar-operated.

---

## Opening (first 10 seconds)

Three moves. Say their name → lead with the observation → end with a question.

1. **Say their name.** "Hey, is this [first name]?"
2. **Lead with the observation, not the offer.** "I was looking at your Google listing this morning and noticed [specific gap — no website / 8 reviews / no review responses]."
3. **Hand them control.** "Is that something you've been meaning to fix, or is it just not a priority right now?"

If they engage past 10 seconds, you've earned the meeting.

---

## Objection 1: "I'm not interested."

**What it is:** Reflexive muscle memory. Not a real objection — they haven't processed what you said.

**Counter:**
> "Yeah, totally fair. I'm not selling anything — I just wanted to flag the [specific gap] because the businesses around you are getting most of the calls because of it. Takes 30 seconds, then you can decide if it's worth your time."

Two moves: agree (removes their pushback energy) + reframe to their problem.

---

## Objection 2: "Just send me an email."

**What it is:** A polite exit. Email-after-this gets opened 5% of the time.

**Counter:**
> "Happy to send it over. Quick question before I do — is the [specific gap] something you'd actually want to fix this month, or are you good with how things are running? Just so I know what to put in the email."

If they say "yeah, I've been meaning to look at that" — you're booking the call, not sending an email.

---

## Objection 3: "How much?"

**What it is:** Buying signal. They're qualifying you. Don't blurt a number.

**Counter:**
> "Good question — depends on the setup. Some of this is a one-time fix, some ongoing. Easiest way is I hop on a quick 15-minute call this week, look at your setup with you, and send you a number that actually fits. Does tomorrow at 10 work, or is later in the week better?"

Three things happening: price feels custom, you signal this isn't templated, you slide into the booking ask.

---

## Booking the Meeting

Use this exact framing when they're engaged past 60 seconds:

> "I'd love to put together a quick walkthrough for your specific setup — what's working, what the gaps are, what fixing them would look like. It's free, takes 15 minutes, and you can decide at the end if you want me to handle it. Does tomorrow at 10am work, or would later in the week be better?"

**Why "walkthrough" not "meeting":** "Meeting" sounds corporate. "Walkthrough" sounds like a favor. Owners agree to walkthroughs.

**Why a specific time:** "Tomorrow at 10" forces yes/no. "What works for you?" forces them to do mental work they won't do for a stranger mid-workday.

**After they say yes:**
1. Confirm date + time out loud: "Great — tomorrow, [day + date], at 10am your time."
2. Get email right then: "What's the best email to send the calendar invite to?"
3. Send the invite within 5 minutes of hanging up.

## Offer Menu (pick ONE per call based on their gap)

| Gap | Offer | Price anchor |
|-----|-------|-------------|
| No website | One-page site: phone, services, hours, quote form. One afternoon build. | $500-1500 |
| Under 30 reviews | Review automation: SMS link after every job, doubles count in 60 days. Full spec: `review-request-product.md` | $297-497 + $97/mo |
| Phone goes unanswered | AI receptionist / missed-call text-back. 62% of SMB calls go unanswered and callers don't call back. | $200-400/mo |

One gap. One fix. Never pitch all three.

**Stack play:** if they have both no website AND low reviews, lead with the website (higher ticket), then upsell review automation as the "while we're at it" add-on. One conversation, two line items.

---

## After the Walkthrough

- If yes → invoice within the hour, start same day, send progress update within 48h
- If "let me think about it" → "What would you need to know to decide today?"
- If no → keep warm, email again in 6 months
- Always: ask for a testimonial + two referrals after delivery
